Flexible Options

Multipurpose furniture and mixed use rooms are trending in 2024. With our changing lifestyles it is no surprise that we need to make the best use of the space in our homes. Dedicated guest rooms have now become multipurpose spaces as a home office by day with a sofabed for overnight guests by night. Lift-top coffee tables in the living room transform into a place for a laptop when needed.
